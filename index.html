Variant Grants

We fund a series of "biology bounties" to move the needle forward in both wet- and computational experiments.

100% of donations go directly into grants. We share all grantee work publicly; in other words, if we pay out a bounty, we also write about it.

We believe in bioabundance. That means more: more people in science, more public understanding, more experimentation. Our first list of bounties will be published soon.
